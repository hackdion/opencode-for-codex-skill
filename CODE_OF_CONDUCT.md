# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project aims to be a respectful, low-drama place to collaborate. We welcome people who are curious, careful, and kind.

Examples of behavior that help:

- being respectful in disagreement
- giving concrete, actionable feedback
- assuming good intent while still being honest
- focusing critique on the work, not the person

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- deliberately hostile or exclusionary language
- publishing private information without permission
- repeated bad-faith arguing or derailing

## Maintainer Responsibilities

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject comments, issues, pull requests, and other contributions that do not align with this code of conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer privately instead of escalating in public. Use the contact path described in [SUPPORT.md](SUPPORT.md). If the issue is security-sensitive, follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) instead.

## Enforcement

Responses may include warning, moderation, temporary restriction, or permanent ban depending on severity and pattern.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies in the repository and in project-related communication channels.
